Small Town Roots

I was born and raised in Lexington, North Carolina, right in the heart of a quiet rural town where southern charm is woven into everyday life. I love wandering through antique stores, searching for rare treasures that carry stories from generations past. Camping with my family is one of my favorite ways to slow down and savor the simple joys: tents under starry skies, laughter by the fire, and the feeling of being surrounded by nature.

Living here also means being surrounded by history. Just two hours away in Asheville stands the grand Biltmore Mansion, a reminder of elegance and southern legacy. Closer to home, the Pisgah Covered Bridge in Asheboro—less than thirty minutes away—offers a glimpse into the past with its rustic beauty. Old Salem in Winston-Salem tells the story of early Moravian settlers, while right in my hometown, the Davidson County Historical Museum preserves the heritage of the place I call home.

Lexington itself is full of southern flavor, from its famous barbecue to the warmth of neighbors who treat you like family. It's a place where front porches still matter, where history whispers through brick streets, and where every corner holds a story worth telling. This small town has shaped me, grounding me in tradition while inspiring me to carry its charm into everything I create.

Creative Passions

"Designing spaces where imagination feels at home."

I’ve been building and creating in Second Life since 2006, and it has become one of my greatest joys. I love getting lost in the details of decorating a sim—choosing textures, colors, and layouts that bring a space to life. Building different structures, from cozy shoreline cottages to grand gathering places, allows me to express my creativity in ways I never imagined. Designing tattoos has been another passion of mine, blending art and identity into something deeply personal, and eventually led me to open Ink My Flesh, my tattoo store.

Opening my own sim and renting parcels to others was a dream realized. It gave me the chance to share my vision of community and shoreline calm with residents who wanted a place to call home. I take pride in creating immersive spaces that feel welcoming, whether it’s a quiet corner for reflection or a lively area for events and gatherings. Every build, every tattoo, every shoreline detail is a piece of me, and I love knowing that others can experience and enjoy the worlds I create.

Vision & Values

"Waterfront Estates is more than land, it's a promise of belonging."

My mission with Waterfront Estates is simple but heartfelt: to offer the most affordable sims for renters all over the world, while creating a community that feels like home. I believe that Second Life should be a place where everyone—no matter their background or budget—can find a shoreline to call their own. That's why I work hard to keep my rentals accessible, without sacrificing beauty, creativity, or comfort.

For me, it's not just about renting land—it's about building belonging. I want every renter to feel welcomed the moment they arrive; to know they're part of something bigger than themselves. Whether they're exploring peaceful parcels, gathering with friends at shoreline landmarks, or simply enjoying the quiet calm of their own space, my hope is that Waterfront Estates gives them joy, connection, and freedom.

I take pride in curating spaces that invite exploration and fun. From cozy corners perfect for reflection to lively areas designed for events and community gatherings, Waterfront Estates is meant to be more than a sim—it's a living, breathing community. My vision is to keep growing this shoreline world, always guided by values of inclusivity, creativity, and care.
